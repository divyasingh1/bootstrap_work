Course Description:


Search Engine Optimization from LearnSocial brings you the firsthand experience of the SEO concepts, which have been globally accepted as the best policies to earn great ranks for hosting your websites with leading search engines like Google, Yahoo, Bing and more.

Though the Search Engine Optimization (SEO) concept is not new, however since its inception in the mid-1990s, it has created huge waves in the world of website hosting and rankings. This strategy involves enhancing the possibilities of a website or webpage being hosted at top rankings on a particular search engine results list. Ideally, the criteria for a website to be ranked on top listings on premier search engine results would depend upon the number of people who visit its web pages. Yet, this approach has been overridden by the SEO technique that effectively optimizes the presence of any website through various promotional methods such as backlinks, Pay per Click (PPC) and other internet marketing campaigns.

The Search Engine Optimization course @ LearnSocial dynamically revolves around the trending best practices which are prevalent in the online marketing platform. We @LearnSocial bring you the latest analysis of how webmasters are creating the need for their websites to perform well on the search engine listings and thereby swelling the traffic on their hosted websites.
